Game Mechanics

Each opponent has different health, attack, and agility (the speed in which they attack).

Click on your opponent to attack him.

If you do not let your attach reach your opponent before starting a new attack, you incur no damage.

The round ends when a character reaches health zero, and all the attacks have concluded.
